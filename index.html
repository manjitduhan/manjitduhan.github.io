Manjit Singh Duhan

I am a Staff Software Engineer at Corning Technologies India, specializing in secure platform software, real-time systems, and embedded infrastructure. With over 14 years of experience across telecom, IoT, and consumer electronics, I design and deliver robust, scalable, and security-first systems that run in production across thousands of devices and edge nodes.

My technical expertise covers the full product lifecycle—from concept and architecture to production deployment and long-term support. I’ve led projects involving IPsec/IKEv2, strongSwan, PKI, OpenSSL, embedded firmware, and cloud-native systems using AWS and containerized edge deployments. Notably, I have built cost-efficient VPN gateways, deployed firmware to 700,000+ IoT devices with OTA, and managed large-scale PKI migrations with zero downtime.
